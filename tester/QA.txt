SECTION 1 — AGREEMENT OVERVIEW & DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.1  PURPOSE

This Service Level Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between FedEx
Corporation ("FedEx") and Horizon Logistics Pvt. Ltd. ("Customer") as of
January 1, 2025. This Agreement governs all shipping services rendered by
FedEx on behalf of Customer and defines performance standards, obligations,
remedies, and liabilities applicable during the effective period.

1.2  KEY DEFINITIONS

SLA:
  This Service Level Agreement — the binding document governing all FedEx
  services for Horizon Logistics Pvt. Ltd.

Business Day:
  Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays and FedEx-observed
  closures. For India-origin shipments, Indian public holidays are additionally
  excluded for local pickup operations.

Shipment:
  Any single package or freight consignment tendered to FedEx for transport
  under this Agreement, identified by a unique FedEx tracking number.

Delivery Commitment:
  The guaranteed delivery date and time window communicated to Customer at
  the time of shipment tender, as confirmed on the FedEx shipping receipt.

Claim:
  A formal written notification by Customer alleging loss, damage, delay, or
  billing discrepancy related to a specific shipment.

Credit:
  A reduction in invoice charges issued by FedEx as remedy for a verified
  service failure, applied to Customer's FedEx account.

Force Majeure:
  Events beyond reasonable control, including acts of God, war, terrorism,
  pandemics, government action, severe weather, or infrastructure failure.

Active Version:
  The most recently executed version of this SLA, which fully supersedes
  all prior versions. Currently: Version 1.0.

Excess Value Coverage:
  Optional declared value protection purchased at time of shipment for
  coverage beyond standard liability limits.

3.2  MEASUREMENT METHODOLOGY

Performance is calculated on a rolling 30-day calendar month basis.
Measurement includes all shipments tendered under the Customer account.
Exclusions from measurement:
  - Force majeure events (documented)
  - Incorrect or incomplete delivery address provided by Customer
  - Recipient unavailability after 2 delivery attempts
  - Customs delays caused by incomplete documentation from Customer
  - Shipments held per Customer instruction

3.3  REMEDY FOR DELIVERY FAILURES

If FedEx fails to meet the on-time delivery commitment on any eligible
shipment, Customer is entitled to a full credit of the shipping charge for
that specific shipment, subject to the claim filing requirements in Section 4.
Credits are applied to account FDX-ACCT-20250047 within 2 billing cycles.

SECTION 9 — TERMINATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9.1  STANDARD TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice
without cause. Notice must be delivered via registered mail or email to the
account contact addresses listed on the cover page.

9.2  TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

Immediate termination is permitted if either party:
  - Materially breaches the Agreement AND
  - Fails to cure the breach within 15 business days of written notice

Material breach examples: repeated non-payment, fraudulent claims, or
persistent SLA violations without good-faith remediation effort.

9.3  SYSTEMIC FAILURE TERMINATION

Per Section 7.1: Customer may terminate with 30-day written notice, without
any early termination penalty, if FedEx fails to meet the Express on-time
delivery commitment for 3 or more consecutive calendar months.

9.4  EARLY TERMINATION FEE

If Customer terminates this Agreement within the first 12 months without
cause or systemic failure grounds:
  Penalty: 10% of annualized shipping spend (based on trailing 3-month average
  multiplied by 4), payable within 30 days of termination date.
  Annualized spend estimate at signing: $480,000 USD
  Early termination fee estimate:        $48,000 USD

9.5  POST-TERMINATION OBLIGATIONS

  - Outstanding claims filed before termination remain valid and are processed
    under this Agreement's terms.
  - FedEx retains all shipment records for 7 years post-termination per federal
    record-keeping requirements.
  - Customer retains access to FedEx Billing Online for 90 days post-termination
    to retrieve historical invoices.
